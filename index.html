How to Import Your Signature

Gmail

  1. Open Gmail and click the gear icon, then "See all settings"
  2. Scroll down to the "Signature" section
  3. Click "Create new" and give it a name
  4. Click inside the signature editor and press Cmd+V (Mac) or Ctrl+V (Windows) to paste
  5. Scroll down and click "Save Changes"

macOS Mail

  1. Open Mail and go to Mail → Settings → Signatures
  2. Select the email account on the left
  3. Click the "+" button to create a new signature
  4. Name your signature in the middle column
  5. Paste your signature in the right panel
  6. Close the settings window to save

iOS Mail

  1. Open Settings → Mail → Signature
  2. If you have multiple accounts, choose "Per Account"
  3. Tap the signature field for your account
  4. Select all existing text and delete it
  5. Paste your new signature (long press and select Paste)
  6. Go back to save automatically
